## 13. Measurement, RACI, Open Decisions, Appendix

### Measurement — the metrics that matter

**North star (proposed):** *Aktivitas offline selesai per anak per minggu.* Bukan MAU atau DAU — karena nilai Kepompong terjadi di luar layar. Metrik ini mengukur apakah aplikasi benar-benar dipakai untuk bonding nyata.

**Leading indicators by AARRR stage:**

| Stage | Leading indicators |
|-------|-------------------|
| Acquisition | TikTok followers, video views, landing page visitors, organic impressions, referral signups |
| Activation | Signup rate, onboarding completion rate, first recommendation viewed, waiting list → beta conversion |
| Retention | Aktivitas selesai/minggu, D7/D30 retention, assessment completion rate, worksheet downloads |
| Referral | Referral signup rate, share-after-value completion, WA group active members |
| Revenue | Trial → paid conversion, MRR, churn rate, ARPU, LTV/CAC |

**Review cadence:**
- **Weekly:** Founder reviews konten performa (TikTok views, blog traffic, signups). Adjust content mix.
- **Monthly:** Funnel review (AARRR), retention curves, Sean Ellis trend (saat beta aktif).
- **Quarterly:** 90-day plan review — what worked, what didn't, what to double down on.

### Open decisions blocking the plan

Ranked by impact:

1. **[HIGH] Kapan MVP benar-benar siap?** — Semua activation dan retention moves bergantung pada timeline MVP. Jika molor, Q2-Q3 target bergeser.
2. **[HIGH] Apakah founder bisa melakukan 34 moves di 90 hari pertama sekaligus ngoding?** — Kapasitas founder adalah bottleneck terbesar. Perlu prioritas ketat atau tambahan tenaga (freelancer konten?).
3. **[MEDIUM] Harga final Premium.** — Fake door test akan menjawab, tapi hasilnya mempengaruhi proyeksi MRR di Q2-Q4.
4. **[MEDIUM] Seed round timing.** — Jika seed close di Q2-Q3, paid layer dan first hire bisa aktif lebih cepat. Jika tidak, semua organik sampai Q4.
5. **[LOW] Collaborator psikolog anak.** — Untuk fitur konsultasi Premium+, perlu jaringan psikolog freelance yang siap.
